Ingredients

  • 60 mL white rum
  • 30 mL soda water
  • 60 mL fresh lime juice
  • 6 mint leaves
  • 2 teaspoons sugar

Instructions

Pour the lime into a glass and mix with 1 tablespoon of sugar. Add some mint leaves and mash them gently against the cup with a spoon. Now, it’s time to fill the cup with ice, add the rum and top up with soda water. Don’t forget to serve with a straw. Perfect recovery after a long day at work! One more thing.
